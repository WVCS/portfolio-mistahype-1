Banana is a Berry vs. Strawberry is not a Berry

Banana Strawberry
  • Develops from a flower with a single ovary
  • Has a soft skin
  • Has a fleshy middle
  • Has small seeds
  • Develops from a flower with multiple ovaries
  • Often found in clusters
  • Categorized as aggregate fruit
